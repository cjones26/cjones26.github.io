About Turo

At Turo, we believe that car rental should be easy, affordable, and personalized. We're here to revolutionize the way people rent cars by connecting car owners with eager renters, creating a community-driven platform that empowers everyone to share their vehicles and unlock new possibilities. Just like Airbnb changed the way people travel and stay, Turo is transforming the way people get around.

Originating in the vibrant city of Charlotte, North Carolina, Turo was founded in 2023 by a group of car enthusiasts who wanted to create a peer-to-peer car rental service that would give individuals the freedom to rent cars on their terms. Since then, we have grown into a global platform, empowering car owners and travelers alike to embark on unforgettable journeys.

Our Unique Approach to Car Rental

What sets Turo apart is our unique approach to car rental. Instead of relying solely on traditional car rental agencies, we've built a dynamic community of car owners who are willing to share their vehicles. Through our platform, you can browse and book a wide range of cars owned by individuals in your local area or even when you're traveling to a new city.

With Turo, you have the freedom to choose the perfect car for your needs. We provide robust search filters, allowing you to customize your search based on criteria such as price, car type, color, and more. Whether you're looking for an economical compact car, a luxurious sedan, a spacious SUV, or even an exotic sports car, Turo has a vehicle to match your preferences and budget.

How Turo Works

Renting a car on Turo is a breeze. Simply create an account, verify your identity, and you'll be ready to explore our vast selection of vehicles. Once you find the perfect car, you can directly contact the owner to discuss the rental details, ask questions, and coordinate the pickup and drop-off arrangements.

When the rental period begins, you'll meet the car owner at the agreed location, complete the necessary documentation, and embark on your journey with peace of mind. Turo offers a comprehensive insurance package to protect both car owners and renters, ensuring a worry-free rental experience for everyone involved.

Join the Turo Community

Turo is more than just a car rental platform; it's a community of passionate car enthusiasts, adventurers, and travelers. By choosing Turo, you're joining a network of like-minded individuals who believe in the power of sharing and making meaningful connections.

Whether you're a car owner looking to earn extra income by sharing your vehicle or a traveler seeking a unique and personalized car rental experience, Turo has something to offer you. Join our community today and embark on a car rental experience that goes beyond the ordinary.

At Turo, we're here to revolutionize car rental, one journey at a time. Let us help you unlock the endless possibilities of sharing and experiencing cars like never before.
